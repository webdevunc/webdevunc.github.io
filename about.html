About Us

Web Development Club at UNC-CH strives to foster a supportive community for web development enthusiasts of all skill levels. We welcome anyone to join us in exploring, learning, and connecting with fellow members. Our goal is to create an environment where members can develop their skills and collaboratively share their passion for web development.
